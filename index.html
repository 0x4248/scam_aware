Lets put a full stop to scams.

Example Scams

Hi mum Im texting you off my friends phone ---- -------- ---- ----------- --------------- ------------ ---- ------- -------- --------------------------- Can you message me on whatsapp i need to sort out the payment.


Hi son, I need some help with a payment. Can you send me your bank details so I can transfer you the money. --- -------- ------------ ------ -------------- ------------ ---- ------- --------------------- ------- --------- -------------------- ---------- --------- ------------- ---- Thanks for the help, dad.


Step 1 Stop.

Stop doing anything the scammer is asking you to do.


Doing this will prevent you from being scammed and losing money.

Step 2 Block.

Block the scammer so they can't contact you again.


You can do this by pressing Block this caller.

Step 3 Report.

Report the scammer to the police or your local authorities.


In the UK you can text

7726
